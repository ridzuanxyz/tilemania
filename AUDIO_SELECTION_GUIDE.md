## üõ†Ô∏è Audio Editing Tips

### If sounds need adjustment:

**Free Audio Editor:** Audacity
- Download: https://www.audacityteam.org/

**Common edits:**
- **Trim**: Remove silence from start/end
- **Normalize**: Make volume consistent
- **Fade In/Out**: Smooth sound transitions
- **Pitch Shift**: Make deselect sound from select sound
- **Export**: Always export as .ogg (File ‚Üí Export ‚Üí Export as OGG)

**Quick fixes:**
- Too loud? Effect ‚Üí Amplify ‚Üí -6 dB
- Too long? Select end ‚Üí Delete
- Need variation? Effect ‚Üí Change Pitch ‚Üí ¬±10%
